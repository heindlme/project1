OMTech is the Organization for Minorities in Technology.

The purpose of OMTech is to:

  • Promote a greater interest in the information technology curricula for women and gender minorities at UWEC
  • Promote communication between women and gender minorities having interest in information tehcnology systems and provide a more comfortable atmosphere for women and gender minorites in technology
  • To provide a welcoming and supportive community to minorities in technology and all others who support minorities in technology
  • To highlight success stories of women and gender minorities in information technology systems through speakers, tours, and letters.
