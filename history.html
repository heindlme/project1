History of OMTech

Our former name is Women in Information Technological Systems. We changed our name to be more inclusive to everyone. The club officially became OMTech September 2019.

Companies that we have toured recently:
  • Jamf
  • Mayo Clinic
Events from Fall 2019:
  • September 18th: Grill Out
    • We had a grill out with yard games to have a meeting where people can network and meet new people.
  • October 2nd: Movie Night
    • We had a movie night where we watched Hidden Figures.
  • October 16th: Homework Help Night and Coloring
    • We had a night to help with homework and we had coloring. There was a representative from Jamf that came to talk about the company.
  • October 30th: Pumpkin Carving
    • We had a fun Halloween themed event with pumkpin carving and candy!
  • November 13th: Game Night
    • We had a game night to allow people to network while having fun!
  • December 11th: Homework Help Night and Holiday Cards
    • We had another homework help night, since it is the week before finals. We also had holiday cards and cookies to decorate.
